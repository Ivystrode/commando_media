Welcome to 29 Commando Online

This website is open to all ranks. Its purpose is for members to share media with everyone else - great pictures they've taken of things they've done or witnessed with the Regiment.

Once your account is approved, you will be able to upload photo albums and pictures, submit ideas, post comments, and view others' profiles to see what they have contributed.

Notices

Staff will post information, updates and announcements in the NOTICES section. Only staff can post notices, but everyone is able to comment.

Ideas

The IDEAS section is open to all ranks to post and comment. All ideas, suggestions and requests are welcome - training, food, accommodation, you name it. Be security aware, or the idea may be removed.

Albums

The ALBUMS section is open to all ranks. This is where you can upload photos and create albums for related photos.

Profiles

Your PROFILE page is your page. Here your latest albums, photos, posts and comments will be displayed for others and yourself to see, as well as some personal information you choose to share.
